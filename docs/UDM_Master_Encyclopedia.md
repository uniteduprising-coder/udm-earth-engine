## How to read this document

Every entry is tagged with a **Tier**. This is not a judgment on the model — it's a provenance label so the 3D engine (and you, six months from now) knows what kind of number you're looking at.

| Tier | Meaning | Engine treatment |
|---|---|---|
| **T1 — Empirically Sourced** | Traces to an external instrument, survey, or measurement independent of the UDM framework itself | Treat as fixed/ground-truth input |
| **T2 — Structural Framework** | Core architecture of your cosmological model (geometry, naming, relationships) as documented in your own research vault | Treat as the engine's world-model skeleton — defines *what exists*, not yet *how much* |
| **T3 — Observational Pattern Match** | Visual/morphological correlation flagged in your own evidence log as requiring quantitative confirmation | Treat as a hypothesis layer — render it, but flag in-UI as unconfirmed |
| **T4 — Simulation Parameter (session-derived)** | A numeric constant generated by inferential bridging from neighboring T1/T2/T3 data, not independently measured. Internally consistent, not externally validated. | Treat as a **tunable engine constant** — required for the simulation to run, not a claim about physical reality |

Nothing here has been deleted or excluded. T4 is exactly as usable for running your engine as T1 — it just shouldn't be displayed to a user (or to future-you) as "confirmed physics" without that label attached.
